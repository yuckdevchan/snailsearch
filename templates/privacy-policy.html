Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 19, 2025

1. Introduction

We value your privacy and are committed to being transparent about the limited data we collect. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you visit our website, how we use it, and your choices regarding that information.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) or use cookies that track you across websites. The only information we store is related to your website experience and preferences.

3. Cookies and Local Storage

We use cookies or local storage only to remember your preferences, such as:

  • Theme settings (light/dark mode)
  • Language preferences
  • Other site appearance or functionality settings

These cookies do not contain or store unique identifiers or any personal information.

4. How We Use the Information

The information stored in your browser is used solely to improve your experience on our website. For example, remembering your preferred theme so you don’t have to reset it every time you visit.

We do not:

  • Track your activity across other websites
  • Sell or share your information with third parties
  • Use analytics services that collect personal data

5. Third-Party Services

Our website does not use third-party cookies or embed third-party services that track or collect your personal data.

6. Your Choices

You can manage or clear cookies and local storage data through your browser settings. However, please note that clearing these may reset your preferences on our site.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. If we make any significant changes, we’ll update the effective date at the top of this page.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, feel free to reach out by leaving an issue on the GitHub repository: https://github.com/yuckdevchan/snailsearch.
